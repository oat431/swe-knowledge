## 4. Interview Script

### 4.1 Introduction (5 minutes)

> "Thank you for taking the time to speak with us today. I'm [Name], and I'm conducting research to understand how you [relevant activity]. 

> This conversation will take about [45 minutes]. There are no right or wrong answers — we're interested in your honest experience and opinions.

> With your permission, I'd like to record this session for note-taking purposes. The recording will only be used internally and won't be shared outside the project team. Do I have your consent?

> You can stop at any time or skip any question you're not comfortable answering. Do you have any questions before we begin?"

### 4.2 Warm-Up (5 minutes)

| # | Question | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | [Tell me about your role. What does a typical day look like?] | [Context setting] |
| 2 | [How long have you been in this role?] | [Experience level] |
| 3 | [What are the main tools/systems you use daily?] | [Tech context] |

### 4.3 Current Behavior (15 minutes)

| # | Question | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 4 | [Walk me through how you currently [relevant task].] | [Current workflow] |
| 5 | [What steps do you take? What order?] | [Process mapping] |
| 6 | [What tools do you use for this?] | [Tool identification] |
| 7 | [How long does this typically take?] | [Time baseline] |
| 8 | [What happens when something goes wrong?] | [Error handling] |
| 9 | [Who else is involved in this process?] | [Stakeholder mapping] |
| 10 | [Can you show me how you do this?] | [Observation] |

### 4.4 Pain Points (10 minutes)

| # | Question | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 11 | [What's the most frustrating part of this process?] | [Pain point identification] |
| 12 | [What takes the most time?] | [Efficiency bottlenecks] |
| 13 | [What do you find yourself doing repeatedly?] | [Automation opportunities] |
| 14 | [Have you found any workarounds?] | [Adaptation patterns] |
| 15 | [What would you change if you could?] | [Desired improvements] |

### 4.5 Needs & Goals (10 minutes)

| # | Question | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 16 | [What does success look like for you in this task?] | [Success criteria] |
| 17 | [What information do you need that you don't have?] | [Information gaps] |
| 18 | [How important is [specific feature] to you?] | [Feature prioritization] |
| 19 | [What would make this process easier?] | [Solution ideas] |
| 20 | [Is there anything else you wish the system could do?] | [Unmet needs] |

### 4.6 Wrap-Up (5 minutes)

| # | Question | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 21 | [Is there anything I didn't ask about that you think is important?] | [Open feedback] |
| 22 | [Would you be willing to participate in future research?] | [Recruitment] |
| 23 | [Do you have any questions for me?] | [Participant questions] |

> "Thank you so much for your time. Your feedback is incredibly valuable and will directly influence how we design the system. Here's your [gift card/incentive] as a thank you."
