## 1. Purpose

> This plan defines strategies for managing stakeholder expectations and engagement throughout the project lifecycle.

## 2. Engagement Strategy

### 2.1 Engagement Levels

| Level | Definition | When to Use |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| **Unaware** | [Does not know about the project] | [Not applicable — all stakeholders should be at least Informed] |
| **Resistant** | [Knows but opposes] | [Needs targeted engagement] |
| **Neutral** | [Knows but neither supports nor opposes] | [Needs motivation] |
| **Supportive** | [Knows and supports] | [Maintain and leverage] |
| **Leading** | [Actively engaged and championing] | [Maximize influence] |

### 2.2 Stakeholder Engagement Assessment

| Stakeholder | Current Level | Desired Level | Gap | Strategy |
|------------|--------------|--------------|-----|----------|
| [Sponsor] | Leading | Leading | None | [Maintain — regular updates, decision involvement] |
| [Business Owner] | Supportive | Leading | ↑1 | [Involve in key decisions, co-present at steering] |
| [Ops Manager] | Neutral | Supportive | ↑1 | [Involve in design, show early wins] |
| [End User A] | Resistant | Supportive | ↑2 | [Address concerns, involve in prototyping, training] |
| [End User B] | Neutral | Supportive | ↑1 | [Regular updates, training, feedback sessions] |
| [IT Director] | Supportive | Supportive | None | [Maintain — monthly technical updates] |
| [Compliance Officer] | Neutral | Supportive | ↑1 | [Compliance reviews, demonstrate audit trail] |
| [Finance Director] | Neutral | Informed | ↑1 | [Monthly financial reports] |

## 3. Engagement Strategies

### 3.1 High Influence / High Interest (Manage Closely)

| Stakeholder | Strategy | Activities | Frequency | Owner |
|------------|----------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| [Sponsor] | [Decision partner] | [Steering committee, 1:1, escalation point] | Weekly | PM |
| [Business Owner] | [Requirements partner] | [Workshops, reviews, sign-off] | Weekly | BA |

### 3.2 High Influence / Low Interest (Keep Satisfied)

| Stakeholder | Strategy | Activities | Frequency | Owner |
|------------|----------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| [IT Director] | [Technical governance] | [Architecture reviews, monthly updates] | Monthly | PM |
| [Finance Director] | [Budget oversight] | [Monthly financial reports] | Monthly | PM |
| [Compliance Officer] | [Regulatory guidance] | [Compliance reviews, audit prep] | Monthly | BA |

### 3.3 Low Influence / High Interest (Keep Informed)

| Stakeholder | Strategy | Activities | Frequency | Owner |
|------------|----------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| [Ops Manager] | [Co-designer] | [Workshops, prototyping, UAT] | Bi-weekly | BA |
| [End Users] | [User validation] | [Workshops, usability testing, training] | Monthly | BA |
| [Customers] | [Customer voice] | [Surveys, UAT] | As needed | BA |

### 3.4 Resistance Management

| Stakeholder | Source of Resistance | Strategy | Owner |
|------------|---------------------|----------|-------|
| [End User A] | [Fear of job displacement, technology anxiety] | [Clarify roles evolve, hands-on training, involve in design] | Change Manager |
| [Ops Manager] | [Concerned about workload during transition] | [Phased approach, protected training time] | PM |
