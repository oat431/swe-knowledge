# Collocations

Collocations are words that naturally go together. Native speakers say "make a decision" — not "do a decision." Using the wrong combination sounds awkward, even if the grammar is correct.

---

## Why Collocations Matter

| Test | How Collocations Are Scored |
|------|----------------------------|
| **IELTS Writing** | Lexical Resource (25% of score) — natural word combinations = higher band |
| **IELTS Speaking** | Lexical Resource — "collocations with flexibility" = Band 7+ |
| **TOEFL Writing** | Language Use — natural phrasing scores higher |
| **TOEFL Speaking** | Language Use — awkward phrasing lowers score |

## The Most Common Collocation Types

### 1. Verb + Noun

The most tested type. Memorize these as pairs.

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| do a mistake | **make** a mistake | ทำผิด |
| do a decision | **make** a decision | ตัดสินใจ |
| do an effort | **make** an effort | พยายาม |
| do progress | **make** progress | คืบหน้า |
| do research | **conduct / do** research | ทำวิจัย |
| make homework | **do** homework | ทำการบ้าน |
| make a crime | **commit** a crime | กระทำความผิด |
| make damage | **cause / do** damage | สร้างความเสียหาย |
| say a lie | **tell** a lie | พูดโกหก |
| say the truth | **tell** the truth | พูดความจริง |
| take attention | **pay / draw** attention | ให้ความสนใจ |
| take a photo | **take** a photo ✅ | ถ่ายรูป |
| take a decision | **make** a decision | ตัดสินใจ |
| give an advice | **give advice** (uncountable!) | ให้คำแนะนำ |
| give an information | **give information** (uncountable!) | ให้ข้อมูล |

### 2. Adjective + Noun

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| strong rain | **heavy** rain | ฝนตกหนัก |
| strong traffic | **heavy** traffic | รถติด |
| strong headache | **bad / severe** headache | ปวดหัวมาก |
| big rain | **heavy** rain | ฝนตกหนัก |
| big problem | **serious / major** problem ✅ (big works too) | ปัญหาใหญ่ |
| deep concerned | **deeply** concerned | กังวลมาก |
| high concerned | **highly** concerned | กังวลมาก |
| fast food | **fast** food ✅ | อาหารจานด่วน |
| fast train | **high-speed** train | รถไฟความเร็วสูง |
| high chance | **good / strong** chance | โอกาสดี |

### 3. Adverb + Adjective

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| very unique | **absolutely / truly** unique | ไม่เหมือนใครจริงๆ |
| very excellent | **absolutely** excellent | ยอดเยี่ยมมาก |
| highly important | **extremely / very** important | สำคัญมาก |
| deeply important | **extremely** important | สำคัญมาก |
| strongly opposed | **strongly** opposed ✅ | คัดค้านอย่างรุนแรง |
| deeply affected | **deeply** affected ✅ | ได้รับผลกระทบอย่างลึกซึ้ง |

> **Rule:** "Very" doesn't pair with extreme adjectives. You can't say "very unique" — it's either unique or it's not. Use "absolutely," "truly," or "completely" instead.

### 4. Verb + Preposition

| ❌ Wrong | ✅ Correct | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| depend of | **depend** on | ขึ้นอยู่กับ |
| consist in | **consist** of | ประกอบด้วย |
| believe at | **believe** in | เชื่อใน |
| suffer of | **suffer** from | ทุกข์ทรมานจาก |
| agree at | **agree** with / on | เห็นด้วยกับ |
| discuss about | **discuss** (no preposition!) | พูดคุยเกี่ยวกับ |
| enter to | **enter** (no preposition!) | เข้าไปใน |
| reach to | **reach** (no preposition!) | ไปถึง |
| mention about | **mention** (no preposition!) | เอ่ยถึง |

### 5. Noun + Noun

| Collocation | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **a sense of** + noun | a sense of **humour / belonging / achievement** |
| **a piece of** + noun | a piece of **advice / information / furniture** |
| **a range of** + noun | a range of **options / products / services** |
| **a number of** + noun | a number of **students / issues** |
| **the majority of** + noun | the majority of **people / cases** |

### 6. Academic Collocations (IELTS/TOEFL Essential)

| Collocation | Meaning | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **conduct research** | do research | Scientists **conducted research** on climate change. |
| **draw a conclusion** | reach a conclusion | We can **draw a conclusion** from the data. |
| **gather evidence** | collect evidence | The police **gathered evidence**. |
| **pose a threat** | create a danger | Pollution **poses a threat** to health. |
| **play a role** | have an influence | Technology **plays a role** in education. |
| **raise awareness** | increase knowledge | The campaign **raised awareness**. |
| **reach a consensus** | agree together | The committee **reached a consensus**. |
| **take into account** | consider | We must **take** cost **into account**. |
| **shed light on** | explain | The study **sheds light on** the issue. |
| **give rise to** | cause | Poverty **gives rise to** crime. |
| **come to a conclusion** | decide | We **came to a conclusion**. |
| **have an impact on** | affect | Technology **has an impact on** society. |

## How to Learn Collocations

1. **Learn in pairs, not singles** — Don't learn "make." Learn "make a decision," "make progress," "make an effort."
2. **Use a collocations dictionary** — Oxford Collocations Dictionary or online: ozdic.com
3. **Read academic texts** — Highlight natural word combinations.
4. **Practice in writing** — Force yourself to use new collocations in essays.
5. **Keep a collocation notebook** — Group by topic (education, health, environment).

## ⚠️ Thai Speaker Traps

| Thai Pattern | ❌ English | ✅ English |
|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| ทำผิด → "do wrong" | I did a mistake. | I **made** a mistake. |
| ฝนตกหนัก → "rain heavy" | There was strong rain. | There was **heavy** rain. |
| ขึ้นอยู่กับ → "depend of" | It depends of the weather. | It **depends on** the weather. |
| พูดคุยเกี่ยวกับ → "discuss about" | We discussed about the issue. | We **discussed** the issue. |
| ให้ข้อมูล → "give an information" | He gave an information. | He **gave information**. (uncountable) |
