# 12 English Tenses

English expresses time through 12 tense combinations: 3 times (Past, Present, Future) × 4 aspects (Simple, Continuous, Perfect, Perfect Continuous). Mastering these is the gateway to fluent English.

---

## The Master Grid

| Form \ Time | **Past** | **Present** | **Future** |
|-------------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Simple** | S + V2 | S + V1(s/es) | S + will + V1 |
| **Continuous** | S + was/were + Ving | S + am/is/are + Ving | S + will + be + Ving |
| **Perfect** | S + had + V3 | S + has/have + V3 | S + will + have + V3 |
| **Perfect Continuous** | S + had + been + Ving | S + has/have + been + Ving | S + will + have + been + Ving |

### Symbols
| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| S | Subject |
| V1 | Base form (go, eat, see) |
| V2 | Past form (went, ate, saw) |
| V3 | Past Participle (gone, eaten, seen) |
| Ving | Present Participle (going, eating, seeing) |

---

## 1. Present Tenses

### Present Simple
> S + V1(s/es)

**Uses:** Facts, habits, routines, general truths.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| I **work** at a bank. | My job (fact) |
| She **works** at a bank. | Her job |
| The sun **rises** in the east. | General truth |
| He **doesn't like** coffee. | Negative |
| **Do** you **speak** English? | Question |

### Present Continuous
> S + am/is/are + Ving

**Uses:** Actions happening NOW, temporary situations, future arrangements.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| I **am writing** a note right now. | Happening now |
| She **is staying** with her friend this week. | Temporary |
| We **are meeting** tomorrow at 3pm. | Future plan |

### Present Perfect
> S + has/have + V3

**Uses:** Past action with present result, life experience, unfinished time.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| I **have lost** my keys. (I still can't find them) | Past → present effect |
| She **has visited** Japan three times. | Life experience |
| They **have lived** here since 2010. | Started past, continues now |

### Present Perfect Continuous
> S + has/have + been + Ving

**Uses:** Action started in past, still continuing, with focus on duration.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| I **have been studying** for 3 hours. | Still studying now |
| It **has been raining** all day. | Still raining |

---

## 2. Past Tenses

### Past Simple
> S + V2

**Uses:** Completed actions at a specific time in the past.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| I **went** to the market yesterday. | Done, finished |
| She **didn't come** to the party. | Negative |
| **Did** you **see** the movie? | Question |

### Past Continuous
> S + was/were + Ving

**Uses:** Action in progress at a specific past moment; interrupted actions.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| I **was sleeping** when you called. | Interrupted |
| At 8pm, they **were watching** TV. | Specific past moment |

### Past Perfect
> S + had + V3

**Uses:** Action completed BEFORE another past action ("the past of the past").

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| When I arrived, the movie **had already started**. | Movie started → I arrived |
| She **had never seen** snow before she moved to Canada. | Before that moment |

### Past Perfect Continuous
> S + had + been + Ving

**Uses:** Action in progress before another past action, emphasizing duration.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| I **had been waiting** for an hour when the bus finally came. | Waiting → bus came |
| They were tired because they **had been working** all day. | Work → tired |

---

## 3. Future Tenses

### Future Simple
> S + will + V1

**Uses:** Predictions, spontaneous decisions, promises.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| It **will rain** tomorrow. | Prediction |
| I'll help you with that. | Spontaneous offer |
| I **won't be** late. | Promise |

### Future Continuous
> S + will + be + Ving

**Uses:** Action in progress at a specific future time.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| This time tomorrow, I **will be flying** to Tokyo. | Specific future moment |
| Don't call at 8 — I **will be eating** dinner. | Future interruption |

### Future Perfect
> S + will + have + V3

**Uses:** Action that will be completed before a specific future time.

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| By next month, I **will have finished** the project. | Project done before next month |
| She **will have left** by the time you arrive. | She leaves before you arrive |

### Future Perfect Continuous
> S + will + have + been + Ving

**Uses:** Duration of an action up to a future point (rare in conversation).

| ✅ Example | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| By December, I **will have been working** here for 5 years. | Duration emphasis |
| Next week, they **will have been traveling** for a month. | Duration |

## Choosing the Right Tense: Decision Flow

```
Is the action...
├── In the PAST?
│   ├── Simple completed? → Past Simple
│   ├── In progress at a past moment? → Past Continuous
│   ├── Before another past action? → Past Perfect
│   └── Duration before another past? → Past Perfect Continuous
│
├── In the PRESENT?
│   ├── Fact / habit / routine? → Present Simple
│   ├── Happening NOW? → Present Continuous
│   ├── Past → present connection? → Present Perfect
│   └── Duration from past → now? → Present Perfect Continuous
│
└── In the FUTURE?
    ├── Prediction / spontaneous? → Future Simple
    ├── In progress at future time? → Future Continuous
    ├── Completed before future time? → Future Perfect
    └── Duration up to future point? → Future Perfect Continuous
```

## ⚠️ Thai Speaker Traps

Thai doesn't conjugate verbs for tense — time is expressed through context words (เมื่อวาน, กำลัง, จะ, แล้ว).

| Thai | ❌ Common Error | ✅ Correct |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| เมื่อวานฉันไปตลาด | Yesterday I go to the market. | Yesterday I **went** to the market. |
| ฉันกำลังกินข้าว | I eating right now. | I **am eating** right now. |
| เขามาถึงแล้ว | He arrive already. | He **has arrived**. |
| ฉันทำงานที่นี่มา 5 ปี | I work here 5 years. | I **have worked** / **have been working** here for 5 years. |

## Quick Test

1. I ___ (live) in Bangkok since 2015. → **have lived**
2. She ___ (watch) TV when I called. → **was watching**
3. By 2030, they ___ (build) the new airport. → **will have built**
4. Water ___ (boil) at 100°C. → **boils**
5. Look! It ___ (rain). → **is raining**
