# Human Computer Interaction

How to design software that humans can actually use. HCI sits at the intersection of psychology, design, and engineering — it's the science behind why some interfaces feel natural and others make you want to throw your computer out the window.

## How They Connect

```
Psychology (Gestalt) → UI Design (Visual execution)
Psychology (UX Laws) → UX Principles (Experience strategy)
                              ↓
                    Human Computer Interaction
```

Gestalt tells you **why** the eye groups things. UI tips tell you **how** to execute it. UX laws tell you **what** users will do. UX principles tell you **how** to design for it.
