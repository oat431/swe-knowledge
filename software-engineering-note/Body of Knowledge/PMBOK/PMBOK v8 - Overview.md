# PMBOK® Guide — Eighth Edition

> Project Management Institute, 2025 | ANSI/PMI 99-001-2025
> Includes The Standard for Project Management | 386 pages

## What Is This?

The **PMBOK® Guide** is the Project Management Institute's (PMI) definitive standard for the project management profession. Now in its eighth edition (2025), it is the most evidence-based revision to date — shaped by nearly 48,000 data points, global practitioner insights from 12 subject matter experts across 10 countries, and two rounds of public feedback yielding more than 12,000 comments.

This volume contains two works in one:

- **The Standard for Project Management** — The ANSI-accredited standard describing the system within which projects operate, the six guiding principles, and the project life cycle framework.
- **A Guide to the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK® Guide)** — Seven performance domains integrating knowledge areas with 40 nonprescriptive processes, plus comprehensive references on tailoring, inputs/outputs, and tools/techniques.

With over 5 million copies in circulation, the PMBOK® Guide remains the premier resource for project professionals worldwide — applicable across all industries, sectors, development approaches (predictive, adaptive, hybrid), and organizational sizes.

## What's New in the Eighth Edition

Since the Seventh Edition (2021), the Eighth Edition presents four categories of change:

- **Key Term and Concept Updates** — Core definitions (project, project management, value) refined for global accessibility, timeliness, and a stronger focus on value delivery over mere output execution.
- **Principle Refinement** — Simplified from 12 principles to **6 actionable principles**, consolidated to minimize overlap, duplication, and confusion.
- **Process Groups Reimagined as Focus Areas** — Five Project Management Focus Areas (Initiating, Planning, Executing, Monitoring & Controlling, Closing) return as practical organizational categories.
- **Updated Performance Domains** — Seven domains integrate Knowledge Areas, processes (40 total), tailoring considerations, and outcome checks — replacing the previous edition's eight domains.

## Core Philosophy

> Project management is the application of knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques to project activities to meet or exceed the intended value.

The PMBOK® Guide — Eighth Edition emphasizes that projects are **investments** undertaken to create value. Success is measured along two dimensions: the efficiency of project management processes (on time, on budget, to standards) and the success of project outcomes (strategic objectives achieved, value realized). The six principles provide the mindset; the seven performance domains provide the mechanics.
