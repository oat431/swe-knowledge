## Protein Cheat Sheet (per serving)

| Food | Amount | Protein | Kcal | Notes |
|------|--------|---------|------|-------|
| Chicken breast | 200g cooked | 60g | 330 | Leanest meat. Grill/boil, don't fry |
| Chicken thigh | 150g cooked | 42g | 320 | More flavor, slightly more fat |
| Eggs | 1 large | 7g | 70 | Boil them in batches for the week |
| Whey protein | 1 scoop (30g) | 25g | 120 | Simplest way to close the gap |
| Greek yogurt | 200g tub | 20g | 130 | Plain, not flavored. Add honey if needed |
| Canned tuna | 1 can (150g drained) | 28g | 150 | In water, not oil |
| Lean beef | 150g cooked | 40g | 380 | 90/10 or leaner |
| Cottage cheese | 200g | 22g | 200 | Good pre-bed snack. Casein digests slow |
| Tofu (firm) | 200g | 18g | 160 | Vegetarian option. Press and pan-fry |
| Milk | 300ml | 10g | 180 | Whole milk if calories allow, skim if tight |
| Pork loin | 150g cooked | 42g | 280 | Lean pork. Trim visible fat |

## Rules

1. **Protein first.** Build every meal around the protein source. Rice/veggies fill the rest.
2. **Meal prep Sundays.** Cook 4-5 chicken breasts and boil 8-10 eggs. You'll hit your numbers without thinking.
3. **Don't drink your calories** (except whey + milk). Sodas, juices, sweet coffee — those are why people miss deficits.
4. **Track for 1 week.** Weigh everything once to calibrate your eye. After that you can go by feel.
5. **If you're hungry at 2,400 kcal:** add 200g vegetables or a protein snack. If you're stuffed: cut the rice portion by 1/4 cup.
