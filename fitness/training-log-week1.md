# Training Log

## How to use
Fill in **Weight** and **Reps** after every set. If you did 3 sets of 8 at 10 kg, write `10 × 8, 8, 8`. If reps dropped (e.g., 8, 8, 6), write that — it tells me where you're failing. Notes column is for anything weird: "felt shoulder," "too easy," "gym packed, used alt."

Bring this log to your next check-in with Coach.
