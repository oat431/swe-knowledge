## Universal "Gym Is Packed" Protocol

If the entire gym is chaos and you can't get anything:

1. **Grab two dumbbells and do this circuit.** 3 rounds, 60 sec rest between rounds:
   - DB Thrusters (squat + OHP) × 10
   - DB Row (each arm) × 10
   - DB Romanian Deadlift × 10
   - Push-ups × 10
   - Plank × 30 sec

2. **Or: bodyweight only.** 4 rounds, 45 sec rest:
   - Push-ups × 12
   - Bodyweight Squats × 15
   - Walking Lunges × 10/leg
   - Inverted Rows (under a table or Smith bar) × 8
   - Dead Bugs × 10/side

Either circuit takes ~20 min. Not ideal, but better than standing around waiting for equipment.

---

## Don't Do This

- ❌ Skip the exercise entirely and go home
- ❌ Wait 15+ minutes for a station — swap immediately
- ❌ Do a completely unrelated exercise because it's the only open machine (e.g., bicep curls instead of squats — no)
- ❌ Change the order so dramatically that you're doing accessories before compounds (one swap is fine; rearranging the whole session isn't)
